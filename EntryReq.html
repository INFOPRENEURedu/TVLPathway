TESDA COVID-19 Certification ▼

The TESDA COVID-19 Certification Online is a free, self-paced e-learning course titled "Practicing COVID-19 Preventive Measures in the Workplace" available via the TESDA Online Program (TOP). It provides essential training on health protocols, including proper PPE usage, hand hygiene, and physical distancing, to ensure safety in the "New Normal." After passing the final assessment, learners receive an electronic certificate of completion, serving as a formal credential for students and workers returning to professional or educational environments.

Self Assessment Guide ▼

TESDA's Self-Assessment Guide is a tool designed to help students evaluate their own skills and readiness for the National Certificate II (NCII) assessment in their chosen Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) strand. It allows learners to identify strengths and areas for improvement by providing step-by-step checklists and competency indicators aligned with TESDA’s standards. Completing the guide is a key requirement for the NCII process, helping students gain a clearer understanding of the skills they need to master before taking the national assessment.

Application Form ▼

The TESDA Application Form (or Learner’s Profile) is the official document required for enrollment in technical-vocational courses or registration for competency assessments. It handles the applicant's personal information, educational background, and work experience to establish a Unique Learner Identifier (ULI). Once processed, the form includes an Admission Slip that serves as the candidate's official permit and schedule for their chosen training or national certification exam.
