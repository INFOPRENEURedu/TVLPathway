JDVP Program

JDVP (Joint Delivery Voucher Program) is a program/initiative by the Philippine Department of Education (DepEd) that provides government funded tuition or assistance vouchers for public Senior High School (SHS) Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) Students track. This allows selected TVL students to partake specialized training at partner private TVI's (Teachnical-Vocational-Institute) such as DATACOM (CSS), International Electronics Technical Institute (EIM), and [institute ng cookery, di ko alam e sorry] (Cookery). This program offers better learning facilities, equipments and a more comprehensive training ground for TVL students to prepare them for their upcoming TESDA National National Assessment.

Free TESDA NC II Assessment

The free TESDA NC II assessment allows TVL students to earn a National Certificate (NC II) at no cost, with all fees fully covered by the national government through a joint effort by TESDA and DepEd.
